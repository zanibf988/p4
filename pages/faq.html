Frequently Asked Questions

What is this website about?

Our platform is a search engine for movies that indexes and organizes links to content already available on the internet. We don't host any files directly but help you find what you're looking for more easily.

Is it legal to download movies from this site?

We only provide links to content that is already publicly available online, similar to how search engines work. The legality of downloading copyrighted content varies by country. We encourage users to check their local laws and support official releases when possible.

What video qualities are available?

You can typically find movies in various qualities including:

  • 480p (SD)
  • 720p (HD)
  • 1080p (Full HD)
  • 2160p (4K) when available

The available quality depends on what's been shared online.

How often is new content added?

We update our index regularly, usually within 24-48 hours after new content becomes available online. Popular movies and shows are typically added fastest.

Why do some links not work?

Links may stop working because:

  • The original uploader removed the content
  • The host site took down the file
  • There was a copyright claim

We regularly clean up broken links and appreciate reports of non-working links.

Do you have subtitles available?

Many of the movies include embedded subtitles (hardcoded). For external subtitle files, we recommend checking dedicated subtitle websites. Some links may include separate subtitle files.

How can I request a movie?

While we don't take direct requests, our system automatically indexes popular content. If a movie is available online, it will likely appear in our index within a few days of release.

Is there a way to support your site?

Yes! You can support us by:

  • Sharing our site with others
  • Reporting broken links to help us maintain quality
  • Donating cryptocurrency (see our Donate page)
  • Disabling ad blockers to support our revenue
Why do I need a VPN?

While not required, we recommend using a VPN for:

  • Added privacy and security
  • Bypassing regional restrictions
  • Protecting against potential ISP monitoring

Some internet providers may throttle or block certain sites.

How can I contact you?

For general inquiries, you can reach us through our Contact page. For copyright-related matters, please see our DMCA page.
