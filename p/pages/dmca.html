About Our Service

This platform operates as an index of links to content that is already available across various internet sources. We function similarly to search engines by:

  • Aggregating publicly available links
  • Organizing content for easier discovery
  • Providing information about media files

We do not upload, host, or store any video, audio, or other media files on our servers. All content remains on third-party platforms.

DMCA Compliance

Although we don't host content directly, we comply with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA) by:

  • Responding to valid takedown notices
  • Removing links to infringing content when properly notified
  • Maintaining a repeat infringer policy

Reporting Copyright Infringement

If you believe your copyrighted work is being linked to without authorization, please send a notice containing:

  1. Your contact information
  2. Identification of the copyrighted work
  3. The exact links on our site you want removed
  4. A statement of good faith belief
  5. A statement of accuracy under penalty of perjury
  6. Your signature (physical or electronic)

Send notices to: dmca@yourdomain.com with "DMCA Notice" in the subject.

Content Removal Process

Upon receiving a valid DMCA notice:

  • We will verify the notice meets all requirements
  • Remove or disable access to the identified links
  • Notify the affected user (if applicable)
  • Document the takedown in our records
